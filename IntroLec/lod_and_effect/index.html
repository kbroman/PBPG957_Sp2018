This is a standard LOD curve plot from QTL analysis, but with links to plots displaying the QTL effects.

Click on a chromosome at the top and a detailed view of the LOD curve for that chromosome is shown on the bottom left.

In the lower-left panel, hover over markers to see names; click to view an effect plot and and phenotype-vs-genotype plot to the right.

In the effect plot, hover over means to see values.

In the pheno-vs-geno plot, hover over points to see individual IDs.
